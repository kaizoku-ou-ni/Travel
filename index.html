Philippines

Itinerary for 7-10 days

Option 1: Palawan (Coron & El Nido)

Coron

  • Do Ultimate Coron Day Boat tour (includes Kayangan lake, Barracuda lake, Twin lagoon, etc.)

El Nido

  • Do Island hopping boat Tour A or Tour C (A is more popular)
  • Watch sunset from Nacpan beach or Marimegmeg beach
  • Optional: Stay at Isla the Experience in Darcotan Island for remote island life experience

Note: El Nido is touristy and pricey compared to other destinations.

Option 2: Coron or El Nido (or both) and Boracay

Boracay

  • Famous for sandy White Beach. It has 4 stations.
  • Station 1- Quiet area, good for accommodation
  • Station 2- Nice restaurants and clubs
  • Station 3 & 4- Water activities (paragliding, paraw sailing, kayaking, jet skiing, etc.)

Note: To reach Boracay, you need to take plane plus ferry (ferry is a short ride). Boracay is even more touristy but since it is a small island it is easy to get around. Suitable for laid back vacation.

Option 3: Cebu & Bohol & Siquijor

Cebu

Note: There is not much to do in Cebu city itself, so head to north or south part of Cebu, which is more than 3 hrs bus ride.
